About Us

Welcome to Oleander Suites Coorg

At Oleander Suites, we offer 9 private suite rooms within a 3 BHK apartment. Each suite is a cozy, private space with its own attached bathroom, queen size bed, flat screen TV, and a convenient tea/coffee maker. As our guest, you'll also have access to a common living room, dining area, and a well-equipped kitchen. It's the perfect set-up for a comfortable and homey stay. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, our suites provide the perfect blend of privacy and shared spaces. We can't wait to welcome you to Oleander Suites for a relaxing and enjoyable experience!
